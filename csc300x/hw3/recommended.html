FOUNTAIN VIEW

Image credit: Spartan Dining


Caf Brunch - $2,832,901,421.00
Spartan Dining: All You Can Eat. Customize your brunch with any of your favorite breakfast items. Try a tasty pork chorizo potato and egg taco, paired with some sausage and a fruit bowl. I have never seen strawberries or blueberries served at the dining hall before and the sausage is doo doo.

Caf Mongolian - $4,413,795,391.00
Spartan Dining: All You Can Eat. Made to order stir fry at the BokChoy station! Fresh vegetables and a protein of your choice cooked right in front of you, served over rice and orange sauce. One of the few decent dining hall dishes that I actually somewhat like.

Caf Treats - $1,302,185,024.00
Spartan Dining: All You Can Eat. Well Spartan Dining didn't post a caption on this one but it looks like an apple desert topped with vanilla ice cream. The deserts are cardboard but who cares about not being allowed to take food out. Always leave with some cookies or a whole pie tray if you're lucky.

SALSARITA'S

Image credit: Madelyn Good


Quesadilla - $10.00+
A soft folded torilla lovingly filled with your favorite fillings and grilled to perfection. I copied that description from their website thank you. Their crusty dried out refried beans are so unappealing, and no matter what fillings you choose their quesadillas are pretty disappointing.

Burrito Bowl - $10.00+
Ditch the tortilla, and fill up a bowl with your favorite burrito ingredients. Burrito bowls are either overflowing yummy goodness or pathetic nothing sadness. $10 for a half filled burrito bowl, no thank you. I'm telling you guys, Salsarita's is just not the sauce. I'm a hater.

Nachos - $10.00+
We smother our wildly addictive tortilla chips in creamy queso and pile it high with your choice of fresh toppings and meat. I agree with that description. They definitely load it up, they like piling everything in the center rather than spreading it around. These nachos are not finger food, use a fork.

INSOMNIA COOKIES

Image credit: Madelyn Good



6 Pack - $10.00
Sit back and relax with a 6-pack of your classic cookie faves. I love cookies hheh. This JavaScript section took me way too long to figure out wtf. Insomnia Cookies are really good if they are baked correctly and freshly served. Also serving vegan and gluten free cookies!

Deluxe Cookie'wich - $8.50
Three scoops of your favorite ice cream between two warm deluxe cookies. I call this one the ice cream burger. I've never tried this monster, but if your eyes are bigger than your stomach, try this and your life will never be the same. Muy delicioso, order once and probably never again.

Brownie - $3.25
Choose between a brownie, blondie, or brookie. A brookie is half cookie, half brownie, a total snack. And my favorite. Feeling loaded? Try a loaded brownie, your favorite brownie topped with frosting and garnished with your favorite toppings. If your brownie is underbaked or rock brick solid, demand a refund.
